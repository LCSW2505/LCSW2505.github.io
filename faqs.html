Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I register for a training session?
A: You can register by visiting our website and selecting the session you wish to attend.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Q: Can I reschedule my training session?
A: Yes, you can reschedule by contacting our support team at least 48 hours before the session.

Q: Will I receive a certificate after completing the training?
A: Yes, all participants will receive a digital certificate upon completion.

Q: Are there any prerequisites for the training?
A: Some courses may have prerequisites. Please check the course details before registering.
